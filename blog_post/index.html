Know Your Land

Pakistan is a country rich with beautiful landscape. Each city has it's own unique aura and serenity.

Lahore

  • Lahore is secomd largest city in Pakistan
  • It is known for its beautiful Mughal-era architecture
  • It has always been a seat of learning and knowledge


Karachi

  • Karachi contains country's most linguistically-, ethnically-, and religiously-diverse regions
  • It is home to country's two largest seaports (Port of Karachi and Port of Bin Qasim)


Quetta

  • Quetta is known for its fruit orchards and vibrant bazaars
  • It is known as the fruit basket of Pakistan
  • It is surrounded by rugged, beautiful mountains


Islamabad

  • Islamabad is the capital of Pakistan
  • It is loved for its calm, peacefull, quiet, and green atmosphere


Swat

  • Swat valley is locally known as Pakistan's switzerland
  • It is famous for its snow-capped mountains and scenic view
